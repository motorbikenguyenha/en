Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a motorbike with Nguyen Ha?

You can book quickly via WhatsApp or Zalo using the links above, or fill out our online form. We confirm availability and arrange delivery within Hanoi for you.

What documents do I need to rent a bike?

Simply present your passport (or local ID) and driver's license. We keep a small deposit and return it when you bring back the bike.

Can I ride outside of Hanoi?

Yes, you can take our bikes outside Hanoi (e.g., to Sapa or Halong). Just let us know your itinerary so we can advise on routing.

How is the payment handled?

We accept cash, bank transfers, or payment via ZaloPay/VNPay. Payment is flexible and can be made online or upon pickup/delivery.

Do you offer long-term rentals?

Absolutely. We have affordable weekly and monthly rates. Long-term customers also get priority maintenance and dedicated support.
