Rental Procedure

We offer two common deposit options for rentals:

  • Passport deposit: Leave your original passport with a valid Vietnam visa as a deposit (common for many travelers).
  • Cash deposit: If you prefer not to leave your passport, pay a refundable cash deposit from 2,000,000 VND to 5,000,000 VND, depending on bike model and rental length.

Full payment is required on handover. Helmets and basic maintenance are included free of charge. Terms & conditions apply — please confirm when booking.
